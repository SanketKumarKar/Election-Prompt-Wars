🚀 **Excited to unveil my latest project: CivicSync!** 🗳️✨

Elections are the cornerstone of democracy, but let's face it—navigating polling locations, understanding complex ballot jargon, and keeping track of the election process can be overwhelming. 

That's why I built **CivicSync**, a comprehensive, AI-powered non-partisan platform designed to empower voters with accessible, understandable, and actionable electoral information! 🌍🙌

**Here is what makes CivicSync special:**
✅ **AI Ballot Demystification:** Uses Google's Gemini AI to translate complex legal jargon into plain, easy-to-understand English with unbiased pros and cons.
✅ **Interactive Indian Election Guide:** An animated, 6-step visual journey explaining how the world's largest democracy votes—from announcement to government formation.
✅ **Smart Polling Locator:** Integrated with Google Maps to help you find your nearest polling booth instantly.
✅ **Multilingual Civic Assistant:** Get your election questions answered in real-time in English, Hindi, and Spanish.
✅ **Voter Roadmap & Cheat Sheet:** Sync election reminders directly to your Google Calendar and build a personalized cheat sheet for voting day.

Built using React, Tailwind CSS, Google Maps API, and the powerful Google Gemini AI! 💻🛠️
